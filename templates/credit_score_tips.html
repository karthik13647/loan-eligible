Credit Score Improvement Tips

  • Pay your credit card and loan EMIs on time.
  • Keep your credit card utilization below 30%.
  • Avoid applying for multiple loans/credit cards at once.
  • Maintain a healthy credit mix (secured + unsecured loans).

What to Avoid:

  • Late EMI Payments
  • Overdrafts and Defaults
  • Closing old credit accounts (hurts credit age)
